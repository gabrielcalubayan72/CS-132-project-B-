About Us

Our data science project, Digging Fake Gold, an analysis of tweets with disinformation on Tallano Gold in the Philippines, in partial fulfillment of the requirements in CS 132. This project aims to see if the 2022 national elections had an effect on the quantity of tweets on Tallano Gold, and draw additional insights from the gathered data.

Motivation

The months leading up to the 2022 National Elections in the Philippines gave rise to a plethora of disinformation on social media.

Problem

Did the 2022 national elections have an effect on the quantity of tweets on Tallano Gold?

Solution

This project will track the timing and quantity of tweets on Tallano Gold, and relate it to the timeline of the 2022 National Elections.

Research Problem

What motivated this project? What is this trying to solve?

Research Question

Did the 2022 national elections have an effect on the quantity of tweets on Tallano Gold?

Alternative Hypothesis

The 2022 Philippine elections (and the months leading up to it) had an effect on the quantity of mis/disinformation tweets on the Marcos family's "Tallano Gold" deposits.

Null Hypothesis

The 2022 Philippine elections had no effect on the quantity of mis/disinformation tweets on the Marcos family's "Tallano Gold" deposits.

Action Plan

Analyze the frequency of mis/disinformation tweets about the Marcos family's "Tallano Gold" deposits.
